# 24H Product Launch Playbook

Rapid digital product validation framework. Ship in 24 hours, spend <$1.

## Constraints
- Time: 24 hours max
- Budget: $1 max (prefer $0.10)
- Must solve real pain point
- Must include payment + delivery

## Product Stack (Free Tier)
| Component | Tool | Purpose |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Product | Notion/Canva/Figma | Content/template/tool |
| Payment | Gumroad | Checkout + delivery |
| Landing | Carrd | Conversion page |
| Distribution | Dev.to, Reddit, IH, HN | Organic reach |
| Asset Gen | KIE.ai, Leonardo, Bing | Thumbnails, visuals |

## Launch Checklist
1. [ ] Product built (2h)
2. [ ] Gumroad listing live (15m)
3. [ ] Thumbnail generated ($0-0.05)
4. [ ] Landing page published (Carrd/Webflow/Netlify)
5. [ ] Dev.to article published (SEO long-tail)
6. [ ] Reddit post (value-first, not promo)
7. [ ] IndieHackers "Show IH" posted
8. [ ] HN "Show HN" posted (low priority)
9. [ ] Twitter/LinkedIn thread posted
10. [ ] Monitor for first sale

## Channel Priority (TOE Framework)
**High Probability (Do First):**
- IndieHackers (founders, buyers)
- Dev.to (SEO traffic)
- Reddit comments (value-first on pain posts)
- Personal networks (LinkedIn, Twitter)

**Low Probability (Skip or Post):**
- HN (algorithm lottery)
- Reddit front page (mod approval)
- Cold outreach (low conversion)

## Learnings
- Carrd account requires email verification (plan for it)
- Gumroad free tier perfect for digital products
- AI-generated thumbnails are commodity ($0.025)
- Dev.to posts get indexed fast (SEO win)
- Reddit: Post from aged accounts, comment from new accounts

## Post-Launch
- Track first sale as validation
- Collect feedback for v2
- Document what worked
- Archive assets for reuse
